Our Heritage

Dressing Royalty. Preserving Culture.

M Sarki Royal Services is dedicated to preserving the timeless beauty of Hausa cultural heritage through master level tailoring and royal embroidery. Every stitch we make is a tribute to dignity, identity, and centuries of tradition.

We craft custom-made traditional outfits for kings, royal personalities, dignitaries, and connoisseurs of premium cultural fashion combining creativity, precision, and an unwavering commitment to quality.

  • Expertise in royal embroidery
  • Custom-made traditional outfits
  • Uncompromising quality & detail
  • Passion for dignity & royalty
